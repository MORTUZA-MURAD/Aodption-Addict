Frequently Asked Questions

Is pet adoption free?

Yes! We believe loving homes shouldn't have financial barriers. Adoption through our platform is completely free.

How do I find a vet?

Use our 'Vet Directory' page to see a list of trusted professionals in your area, or use the Emergency button for urgent care.

Is there a mobile app?

We are currently developing mobile apps for iOS and Android, scheduled to launch in late 2024.

How does fostering work?

Fostering provides a temporary home. We supply food and medical care while you provide love and shelter.

About Adoption Addict

At Adoption Addict, we are more than just a platform; we are a movement dedicated to rewriting the stories of homeless pets. Our mission is to bridge the gap between compassionate hearts and wagging tails, ensuring that every adoption is a seamless journey of love and connection. We leverage technology to simplify the adoption process, provide essential resources for new pet parents, and build a vibrant community where unconditional love finds its forever home.

What We Do

  • Pet Matching Service: Our unique algorithm helps match your lifestyle with the perfect pet personality.
  • Adoption Portal: Browse hundreds of pets from shelters and rescue organizations nationwide.
  • Community Events: Adoption fairs, training workshops, and pet-friendly gatherings.
  • Low-cost Vet Clinic: Affordable healthcare services ensuring every pet stays happy, healthy, and vaccinated without breaking the bank.
  • Lost and Found Pet: A dedicated community network and alert system to help reunite lost furry friends with their worried families.
  • Pet Care Assistance: Comprehensive guidance, advice, and support for both first-time owners and seasoned pet parents.
  • Pet Resource Center: A one-stop hub for training tips, nutritional advice, behavior guides, and emergency contacts.
